How it all began..

In April 2016, my boyfriend and I were lucky enough to get a plot at a community garden in South Austin. After a trip to the garden festival and to the The Natural Gardener we were in business. This is the first garden I've ever had! We knew that growing things in Texas would be very different from growing things in Virginia, where we recently moved from. As soon as we bought some transplants, we got digging!

What is a community garden anyway?

Community gardens are organized in various ways. The one I belong to has individual plots, as well as some community plots. We pay a small fee every few months and complete work hours to keep our plot. For work hours, sometimes I weed or mow the common areas of the garden. Everyone is free to grow whatever they want to in their own individual plot.

Best parts of having a garden

  • Fresh vegetable year-round
  • An easy way to connect to your food
  • Great way to relieve stress
  • An excuse to get outdoors
  • It is beneficial to the environment
